The Problem

Your targeting is broken.
Here's why.

Most B2B pipeline efforts are targeting the wrong accounts at the wrong time with messages that sound like everyone else's. These are the three patterns we see killing pipeline.

Wrong accounts, wrong timing

Your SDRs are working lists built on firmographics — right industry, right size, right title. But that tells you nothing about timing. You're reaching companies that technically fit but have zero urgency right now.

What changes: Signal-based targeting identifies accounts showing actual buying indicators — leadership changes, funding, tech stack shifts, expansion signals. The right target list also dramatically reduces wasted spend across your paid channels.

Every outreach sounds the same

AI-personalised outreach was a competitive advantage for about six months. Now every channel is flooded with "I noticed your company..." messages. Response rates are dropping because the space is crowded with better-sounding noise.

What changes: Outreach built around pain point segments, not personalisation tokens. Every account matched to a specific friction point your product solves.

Tools but no system

You've bought Clay, or Apollo, or a sequencing tool. Maybe all three. They're sitting half-configured because nobody has the time or expertise to build a proper pipeline from data to meeting. You're paying for infrastructure that isn't producing.

What changes: One integrated system — research, enrichment, scoring, segmentation, copy, and execution — built and operated by a team that does this every day.
